How it works
Three steps.
Total freedom.
01
Connect your accounts
Link Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, WhatsApp and Messenger in seconds. One secure login per platform via OAuth.
02
Write once
Compose your post, message or update in WyreIt's clean editor. Add photos, videos or links just like you normally would.
03
WyreIt everywhere
Choose your platforms with a tap and broadcast instantly. All platforms update simultaneously — no copy-pasting, no app switching.
Features
Everything in
one signal.
One-tap broadcast
Post to every platform simultaneously. Toggle platforms on or off per post — full control, zero friction.
Unified messaging
Send DMs via Messenger, Instagram, X and WhatsApp from one inbox. One platform or all at once.
Unified feed
All your platforms in one scrollable timeline. Filter by platform or see everything together.
Photo & video
Upload once, publish everywhere. WyreIt handles format optimisation per platform automatically.
Schedule posts
Plan ahead. Schedule any post to go live across all platforms at exactly the right time.
Private & secure
Your credentials never leave your device. OAuth authentication, zero data selling, ever.
