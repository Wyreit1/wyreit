The short version WyreIt connects your social media accounts so you can post and message from one place. We do not sell your data. We do not read your private messages. We store only what is essential to make the app work, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1
Who we are

WyreIt is a social media management application operated by WyreIt Ltd, registered in England and Wales. Our registered address and contact details are listed at the end of this policy.

For the purposes of UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018, WyreIt Ltd is the data controller for personal data collected through this application and website (wyreit.app).

2
What data we collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the WyreIt service. This falls into three categories:

  • Account information: Your name, email address and password (stored in encrypted form) when you register for WyreIt.
  • Platform connection tokens: OAuth access tokens that allow WyreIt to post and retrieve content on your behalf from connected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X, TikTok, WhatsApp, Messenger). These tokens are encrypted and never stored in plain text.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics such as which features you use and when you log in. This helps us improve the app. It does not include the content of your posts or messages.
  • Device and technical data: Your IP address, browser type, and operating system, collected automatically when you access wyreit.app. Used for security and performance purposes only.
  • Waitlist data: If you joined our waitlist, we hold your email address solely to notify you of the app launch.
What we do NOT collect We do not read, store, or analyse the content of your private messages. We do not retain copies of the posts you publish through WyreIt after they have been sent to the relevant platforms. We do not collect sensitive personal data such as health information, political views, or financial data.

3
How we use your data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes, each with a lawful basis under UK GDPR:

  • To provide the service (Contract): Authenticating your account, connecting to your social platforms, and enabling you to post and message across platforms.
  • To improve the app (Legitimate interests): Analysing usage patterns to fix bugs, improve features, and make the service faster and more reliable.
  • To contact you (Consent or Contract): Sending you important service updates, security alerts, or — with your explicit consent — product news and updates. You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time.
  • To comply with legal obligations (Legal obligation): Retaining records where required by law and responding to lawful requests from authorities.

4
Third-party platforms

WyreIt connects to third-party social media platforms on your behalf. When you authorise a connection, you are granting WyreIt permission to act on your behalf within the limits of the permissions you approve via that platform's OAuth flow.

Each platform has its own privacy policy and terms of service, which apply independently of WyreIt. We recommend you review them:

  • Meta (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp): www.facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • X (Twitter): twitter.com/en/privacy
  • TikTok: www.tiktok.com/legal/page/uk/privacy-policy

WyreIt only requests the minimum permissions necessary to post and message on your behalf. We never request access to your friends lists, advertising data, payment information, or any permissions beyond what the core service requires.

5
Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Full stop. WyreIt does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for advertising or commercial purposes.

We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate WyreIt (e.g. cloud hosting, error monitoring). These providers are contractually bound to process your data only as instructed by us and in line with UK GDPR.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority, we may disclose your data. We will notify you where legally permitted to do so.
  • Business transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of WyreIt Ltd, your data may be transferred to the acquiring party. You will be notified in advance and given the option to delete your account.

6
Data storage & security

Your data is stored on servers located within the United Kingdom and European Economic Area. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit: All data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit.
  • OAuth token security: Platform access tokens are encrypted before storage and never exposed in logs or error reports.
  • Access controls: Only authorised WyreIt personnel can access production systems, and only where operationally necessary.
  • Regular security audits: We conduct regular security reviews and penetration testing of our infrastructure.

No system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by UK GDPR.

7
How long we keep your data

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • OAuth tokens: Deleted immediately upon disconnecting a platform or closing your account.
  • Usage logs: Retained for up to 12 months, then anonymised or deleted.
  • Waitlist emails: Retained until the app launches and you have been notified, or until you request removal.
  • Legal records: Where we are required by law to retain data, we keep it only for the minimum legally required period.

8
Your rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data. You can exercise any of these by contacting us at the address below:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data. We will delete all data within 30 days unless we have a legal obligation to retain it.
  • Right to restrict processing: Ask us to pause processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests, including for direct marketing.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been processed unlawfully.

We will respond to all data rights requests within 30 days.

9
Cookies

WyreIt uses a minimal number of cookies to keep the service functional:

  • Essential cookies: Required for you to stay logged in and use the app. Cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage data to help us understand how the app is used. You may opt out of these at any time via your account settings.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing cookies. We do not participate in cross-site tracking.

10
Children's privacy

WyreIt is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account and all associated data without delay.

Users aged 13–17 must have parental consent to use WyreIt where required by applicable law.

11
Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the law, our practices, or the services we offer. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify you by email at least 14 days before the changes take effect
  • Where required by law, ask for your renewed consent

Your continued use of WyreIt after the effective date of any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
